This visualization shows the hourly pedestrian density on a certain street, day, and hour in Melbourne, Australia for the month of November, 2019. The hexagon layer hovers over a street and its height and color presents the pedestrian count for that specific street. By hovering over a hexagon you will be greeted with more information for that street. You can also filter by date and hour using the filter box.
